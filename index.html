Frequently asked questions

How do I verify a roofer is licensed and insured in Mesa, AZ?

Ask for the contractor's Arizona ROC license number and proof of general liability and worker's compensation insurance. Verify the ROC license at the Arizona Registrar of Contractors website and confirm insurance coverage with the provider before work begins.

What should I expect during a professional roof inspection?

A thorough inspection includes checking shingles or tiles, flashing, valleys, gutters, attic ventilation, and signs of water intrusion. The roofer should provide a written report with photos, prioritized recommendations, and a cost estimate for repairs or replacement.

Will local roofers know how to handle monsoon and heat-related damage?

Yes. Experienced Mesa roofers understand monsoon-related wind and hail damage as well as heat-related wear. They can recommend materials and installation techniques that improve durability and reduce long-term maintenance in Arizona's climate.
